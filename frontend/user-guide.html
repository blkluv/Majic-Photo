1. Getting Started - Registration

Creating Your Account

  1. Visit Majic-Photo.com and click "Get Started" or "Start Free"
  2. Fill in your email address - this will be your login username
  3. Choose a secure password for your account
  4. Enter your company name (optional, but helpful for organization)
  5. Registration Code (optional): Leave blank for 10 free photos, or enter a 32-character code for unlimited access
  6. Click "Create Account"

üí° Registration Code Information

  • Without a code: You get 10 free photo enhancements to test the service
  • With a code: Unlimited photo processing with all premium features
  • Code format: 32-character hexadecimal string (e.g., "a1b2c3d4e5f6...)

4. AI Processing and Results

What Happens During Processing

  1. Image Classification:

    AI analyzes each photo to determine if it's an exterior, empty interior, or cluttered interior

  2. Room Context Analysis:

    The AI considers the room name you provided to choose appropriate furniture and decor

  3. Virtual Staging:

    AI adds realistic furniture, lighting, and decorative elements while preserving architectural features

  4. Processing Status:

    Photos show "Processing..." status and update automatically when complete (typically 30 seconds)

‚ú® AI Staging Features

  • Room-appropriate furniture: Kitchens get dining sets, bedrooms get beds, etc.
  • Realistic proportions: Furniture is properly scaled to room size
  • Preserves fixtures: Never modifies built-in features, fireplaces, or permanent installations
  • Professional quality: High-resolution output perfect for MLS listings

5. Reprocessing with Custom Prompts

If the initial AI result doesn't meet your expectations, you can reprocess the photo with custom instructions.

How to Reprocess a Photo

  1. Find the Reprocess Link:

    Look for the small "Reprocess" link under enhanced photos

  2. Choose Source Image:

    Select whether to reprocess from the original (start fresh) or enhanced (refine existing) version

  3. Write Custom Prompt:

    Describe the specific changes you want (see examples below)

  4. Submit for Processing:

    Click "Reprocess Photo" to start the new enhancement

üí° Custom Prompt Examples

"Add modern minimalist furniture with white and grey colors"

"Stage this bedroom with a king-size bed and luxury bedding"

"Add warm, cozy furniture suitable for a family living room"

"Stage with contemporary dining furniture and pendant lighting"

‚ö†Ô∏è Reprocessing Notes

  • Reprocessing uses one of your photo credits
  • You can reprocess the same photo multiple times
  • Processing time is typically 30-60 seconds
  • The reprocessed version will replace your previous enhanced image

Step 1: Click "Reprocess" link under any enhanced photo (in this case, the doorway was covered up in the enhanced photo)

Step 2: Choose source image to reprocess and enter custom prompt to describe specific changes you want completed

8. Tips for Best Results

‚úÖ Photography Best Practices

  • Shoot during golden hour or with good natural light
  • Use a wide-angle lens to capture entire rooms
  • Keep photos horizontal/landscape orientation
  • Remove personal items and clutter before shooting
  • Ensure good exposure - not too dark or bright

üéØ Room Naming Tips

  • Be specific: "Master Bedroom" vs "Bedroom"
  • Include function: "Dining Room", "Home Office"
  • Mention size if relevant: "Large Living Room"
  • Use standard terms AI recognizes

üîÑ Reprocessing Strategies

  • Start with "Original" for completely different styles
  • Use "Enhanced" for minor adjustments
  • Be specific in prompts: colors, styles, furniture types
  • Try different prompts if first attempt isn't perfect

‚ö° Workflow Efficiency

  • Process multiple photos from same property together
  • Use consistent room naming across properties
  • Take advantage of filtering to find photos quickly
  • Download enhanced photos for your MLS listings
